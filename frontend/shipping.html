Shipping FAQs

Is it safe to ship puppies?

Absolutely! We use certified pet-safe airlines and professional pet transport services with decades of experience. Your puppy travels in climate-controlled, pressurized cargo holds with constant monitoring.

What does shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary by region. USA shipping typically ranges from $200-$400, Canada $300-$500, and Europe $400-$600. We provide a quote during your booking consultation.

Can I pick up my puppy in person?

Yes! If you're able to travel to us, we offer pickup options at a discounted rate. Contact us to arrange this option.

What if my puppy gets sick during shipping?

Our puppies are thoroughly health-checked before shipping. We provide travel insurance and have emergency vet contacts in all major airports along the route.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select countries in Europe, Canada, and the USA. We handle all international documentation, customs, and quarantine requirements.
