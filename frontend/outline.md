## Page Content Structure

### 1. Homepage (index.html)
- **Hero Section**: Large background image with Kami Kennels branding
- **Mission Statement**: Professional breeding philosophy
- **Featured Puppies**: Carousel of available puppies
- **Quick Stats**: Years of experience, puppies placed, etc.
- **Call-to-Action**: Links to available puppies and contact

### 2. About Page (about.html)
- **Breed History**: Karelian Bear Dog origins and characteristics
- **Our Story**: Kami Kennels background and philosophy
- **Breed Standards**: Size, temperament, appearance details
- **Why Choose Karelian Bear Dogs**: Benefits and considerations

### 3. Available Puppies (puppies.html)
- **Puppy Gallery**: Grid of 12+ puppies with photos
- **Puppy Profiles**: Name, age, gender, personality traits
- **Adoption Process**: Step-by-step guide
- **Contact Buttons**: Direct links to inquiry form

### 4. Blog Page (blog.html)
- **Recent News**: Updates about the kennel
- **Success Stories**: Adoption testimonials
- **Training Tips**: Karelian Bear Dog care guides
- **Health Information**: Breed-specific health topics

### 5. FAQ Page (faq.html)
- **General Questions**: About the breed and adoption
- **Care Instructions**: Feeding, exercise, grooming
- **Health Concerns**: Common issues and prevention
- **Training Advice**: Behavioral guidance

### 6. Contact Page (contact.html)
- **Branch Information**: Canada, US, Europe locations
- **Contact Form**: Name, email, phone, message
- **Business Hours**: Availability by location
- **Maps/Directions**: Location details
