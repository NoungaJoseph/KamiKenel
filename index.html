Our Mission

At Kami Kennels, we are dedicated to preserving and enhancing the exceptional qualities of the Karelian Bear Dog breed through responsible breeding practices, comprehensive health testing, and meticulous care for our dogs.

Health & Quality

Comprehensive health testing and genetic screening to ensure the highest quality puppies with excellent long-term health.

Temperament Focus

Careful selection and socialization to produce puppies with stable, confident temperaments suitable for family companionship.

Lifetime Support

Ongoing guidance and support for our puppy families throughout their dog's life, from training advice to health consultations.
